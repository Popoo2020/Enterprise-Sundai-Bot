HUMAN-CENTRED · ETHICAL · PROFESSIONAL

Responsible AI begins with people, accountability and evidence.

SundAI combines technical security with organisational reality and human impact. The objective is useful AI that remains understandable, contestable and under meaningful human control.

01

Human oversight

People retain decision rights, escalation paths and the ability to challenge, correct or stop AI-supported outcomes.

02

Fairness & accessibility

We consider who may be excluded, disadvantaged or unable to understand and contest a process.

03

Security & privacy

Data minimisation, access control, supplier review, monitoring and safe fallback are designed into the workflow.

04

Transparency

Owners, users and affected people should know what the system does, its limitations and where responsibility sits.

05

Measured value

Use cases are prioritised by business value, feasibility, human impact and risk—not novelty alone.

06

Continuous governance

AI changes over time. Controls, evidence, incidents, suppliers and model behaviour require ongoing review.

STANDARDS-ALIGNED

Professionally grounded, without overstating certification.

Our work is informed by the EU AI Act, ISO/IEC 42001 AI management principles, ISO/IEC 27001 security practices and role-based AI literacy. SundAI does not claim organisational ISO certification unless a current certificate explicitly covers the organisation and scope.
