Use case · AI governance

Understand shadow AI before it becomes hidden operational risk.

Map how people actually use AI and replace uncertainty with clear, proportionate guidance.

When this is useful

Employees use personal AI accounts, sensitive data may be copied into tools, or policy and reality no longer match.

What SundAI delivers

  • AI-use discovery and stakeholder interviews
  • Data and confidentiality risk review
  • Approved-versus-unapproved tool map
  • Proportionate acceptable-use guidance
  • Reporting, escalation and learning plan

What you leave with

A realistic operating picture and a safer path that supports productivity instead of driving use further underground.

How do you identify shadow AI?

Through interviews, surveys, workflow mapping, approved-tool review and available technical evidence.

Can the review protect employee trust?

Yes. The process can be transparent, proportionate and focused on work practices rather than blame.

What happens after the review?

Organisations receive priorities for approved tools, policy, training, controls and follow-up governance.
