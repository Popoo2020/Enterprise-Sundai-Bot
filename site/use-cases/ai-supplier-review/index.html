Use case · Supplier risk

Ask the right questions before an AI supplier becomes a dependency.

Connect procurement, privacy, security, governance and business ownership in one practical review.

When this is useful

You are buying an AI platform, enabling a new feature, renewing a supplier or connecting sensitive organisational data.

What SundAI delivers

  • Data-use and retention questions
  • Security, access and incident review
  • Model and subprocessor dependencies
  • Governance, monitoring and change controls
  • Prioritised decision and follow-up questions

What you leave with

A documented, risk-based view of what is acceptable, what needs mitigation and what requires further legal or technical review.

Can you review an existing supplier?

Yes. Existing platforms can be reviewed when configurations, features or organisational use have changed.

Can the review support procurement?

Yes. Findings can be translated into supplier questions, approval conditions and ownership actions.

Is the same depth needed for every tool?

No. The review should be proportionate to data sensitivity, decision impact, scale and dependency.
