FREE PRACTICAL RESOURCES

Useful starting points for responsible AI work.

Concise templates to help European organisations ask better questions before buying, deploying or scaling AI.

01

AI Governance Checklist

  • List systems, embedded AI and informal use
  • Name business and technical owners
  • Classify purpose, people affected and risk
  • Define approval, monitoring and incident paths
  • Record suppliers, data and evidence
For leadership, compliance and risk teams
02

Responsible AI Use Review

  • Identify tools already used by teams
  • Map sensitive information entered or uploaded
  • Check account ownership and access
  • Assess output verification and human reliance
  • Decide whether to approve, restrict or replace
For IT, operations and people teams
03

AI Supplier Review Questions

  • Data location, retention and model training
  • Identity, access and tenant separation
  • Logging, incidents and subcontractors
  • Testing, quality and human oversight
  • Exit, deletion and continuity arrangements
For procurement and renewal decisions

USE WITH JUDGEMENT

Templates support decisions; they do not replace context.

Every organisation, sector and use case has different legal, technical and human-impact requirements. Use these resources as a starting point and document why decisions were made.
